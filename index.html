Who Are We

Sunwood, based in Liaoning, China, is a leading supplier of premium Chinese classical rosewood furniture and fine handcrafted rosewood gifts.

We operate a showroom exceeding 100,000 sq ft and have been dedicated to fine wood craftsmanship for over two decades, blending traditional Chinese aesthetics with modern elegance.

Sunwood owns several original design patents for rosewood gift collections and operates its own manufacturing facilities, ensuring exceptional quality and authenticity in every creation.

The Story Behind Our Logo

Our logo is inspired by the natural growth rings of wood — symbolizing sustainability, harmony, and the enduring spirit of craftsmanship. The triangular form represents balance between enterprise, customers, and employees.

Each flowing line stands for vitality and our commitment to environmental protection and responsible timber sourcing.
